Nemesis Gym Tepeyac Website Plan

Site Name

Nemesis Gym Tepeyac

This name was selected because it directly represents the gym and its location, making it easy for current and future members to identify the business.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about membership plans, fitness classes, personal training services, workout programs, schedules, and contact information. It will help users learn about the gym and support a healthy lifestyle.

Scenarios

  • What membership plans are available and what do they include?
  • What fitness classes are offered and when are they scheduled?
  • How can I contact a trainer or schedule a personal training session?

Color Scheme

Dark Red (#B22222)

Used for headings, buttons, and navigation.

Black (#1A1A1A)

Used for footer and background sections.

Light Gray (#F5F5F5)

Used for page backgrounds and content sections.

Typography

Poppins - Headings and navigation.

Roboto - Body text and paragraphs.

Oswald - Promotional banners and special highlights.
